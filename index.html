      The following documents are the first of eleven pairs to be published, each pair containing Astral experiments with two genii of the Domes of Liber CCXXXI. The Operator makes no apology for the several overt mistakes, blunders & delusions encountered in these records-- on the contrary, they are part of the will of their publication. Beginning students of Astral sight & travel may benefit to see a record of what not to do, and to witness the ever increasing refining of technique and deepening of contact.

     It should also be noted that all known records of other experiments in Liber CCXXXI indicate Operators beginning at the most 'dense' path of TAU, the one 'closest' to the realities of MLKVTh... not so with this current record, that of the 'hanged beast' 999'.'! He began from the inside-out, starting at ALPh & working his way 'down'. With such a journey it has been stated that this may have been a mistake, from the point of view of economy-of-motion-- for the Operator had to get his 'sea-legs' in the refined, subtle and supernal paths rather than in the overt brutality of the Universe. Let success be the proof.
