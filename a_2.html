Changes to Cave-Temple:

  • Tablets of Enoch are set about quarters. I switch the North for theEast when I am working in there, as these are another’s tablets. [They use the old quarter-attributions, whereas I use those of Liber V.]

  • I shall put my Ganesha away for this opus. Luna is airy, but Ganesha reminds me of a Martial-Luna, not Air.
  • Alter bare, save the black cover-clothe & the candle—my dagger, & the sigil of Aou-iao-aou of course. & also:

  • Fool, Atv 0, on Alter. Veiling at first the sigil of the geni til the preliminary invocations are completed. At the point just prior to ‘launch’, Operator will unveil the sigil.
  • Wooden Stele, true size, set in the quarter of Boleskine:N.E. (this too is another’s, but in the common-temple for all)

    In astral, launched body of light; up. up. up. Rising. Intoned Geni’s name ("G.N." hereafter) drew sigil. Kept rising. Intoned G.N., drew sigil.

    Voices: [gibberish, but seer sensed he had ‘disturbed’ something.]

    Seer re-invoked. Saw machinery vision; heard click clacks. Drew banishing Air-Pentagram, dissolved back into flashing LVX of Rising, as before. Seer re-invoked Air-Pentagram, intoned G.N.

    Voice: [a wonderful poem of gibberish, rhyming. Unrecalled.]

    Seer banished via Air-Pentagram. Dissolved.

    Voice: "I am here."

    Seer tested--repeated intonation of G.N. & drew sigil [of Geni].

    Seer: "What is thy function in linking ‘1’ to ‘2’ ?"

    Voice: "I bridge KThR with the Crown."

    Seer tested. Accepted.

    Seer: "Thou art the Thunderbolt of the Swastika?"

    Voice: [Angry tone] "I am the Thunderbolt!"

    Tested. Scrambled into noise. Reject.

    Re-invoked with [G.] sigil & intoned G.N.

    Voice: "[rhyming poem, unrecalled now.]

    Rebanished via Air-Pentagram; re-invoked sigil & G.N.

    Seer: "Illustrate for me the formula of Yetzirah, of the Formative astral."

    ...saw vision of machinery, pistons, belts, conveyers, cutters, factory-line workers moving in unison. Tested. More images of repetitive actions & heard ‘tick tock tick tock’ in sync with repetitive actions. Tested with Invoking Pentagram of Air.

    Voice: "I am the balance of motion - formative actions in mind."

    Tested. Remained, heard chuckling. Constrained geni with astral dagger.

    Seer: "Indwell now thy sigil, Aou-iao-aou indwell thy seal."

    Seer locked into position facing Boleskine, did Greek exorcism, finger-dagger pointing at seal.

    Felt geni indwell therein. Sealed via banishing Pentagram of Air over seal.

    Body of light thence returned unto GVPh. Assumed H[oor]P[aar]K[raat].
